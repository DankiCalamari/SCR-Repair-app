Warranty Terms & Conditions

What is Covered

  • Workmanship defects related to the original repair for a period of 90 days from the date of repair completion.
  • Replacement parts installed by Sunset Country Repairs that fail under normal use within the warranty period.

What is NOT Covered

  • Physical damage (cracked screens, bent frames, water damage) occurring after repair completion.
  • Liquid damage of any kind.
  • Software issues, viruses, or data loss unrelated to the original repair.
  • Unauthorized modification or third-party repair attempts.
  • Pre-existing faults not related to the original repair.
  • Normal wear and tear, including battery degradation.
  • Accessories or peripherals not supplied by Sunset Country Repairs.

How to Make a Claim

Contact Sunset Country Repairs with your warranty number and a description of the issue. The device must be returned for inspection before any warranty work is undertaken. Proof of warranty (this certificate or SMS/Email confirmation) is required.
